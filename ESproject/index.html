About EcoVision

EcoVision is a public awareness project designed to educate people about environmental protection in villages and rural areas. Rapid industrial growth, pollution, water scarcity, and deforestation are affecting our environment badly.

Our goal is to encourage people to use eco-friendly methods such as organic farming, rainwater harvesting, solar energy, recycling, and tree plantation.

Major Environmental Issues

Water Scarcity 💧

Villages suffer from lack of clean water because of groundwater depletion and climate change.

Soil Pollution 🌱

Excessive use of chemicals and fertilizers damages soil fertility and reduces crop quality.

Deforestation 🌳

Cutting trees causes global warming, loss of wildlife, and reduction in rainfall.

Air Pollution 🌫

Burning waste and industrial smoke pollute air and affect human health.

Solutions For Better Environment

🌧 Rainwater Harvesting

Collecting rainwater helps save water for farming and daily use.

☀ Solar Energy

Solar panels reduce electricity bills and pollution.

♻ Recycling

Recycling plastic and paper reduces environmental waste.

🌾 Organic Farming

Organic farming protects soil and produces healthy crops.
